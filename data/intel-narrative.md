## Key Observations

- **Platner raised $7.87M** through Dec 31, 2025 — overwhelmingly small-dollar grassroots: 99% of donations under $200 (avg $25.88).
- **Collins holds cash advantage** ($8.0M vs $3.7M) but faces structural headwinds with -30 net unfavorable among independents.
- **Senate Leadership Fund** committed ~$42M in outside spending to defend Collins — the largest outside commitment in the race.
- **Platner leads every public primary poll**: 55-28 (Emerson Mar 2026), 64-26 (UNH Feb 2026), 66-28 (Impact Research Mar 2026).
- **General election**: Platner ahead of Collins 48-41 (Emerson), 49-38 (UNH), 44-40 (Pan Atlantic).
- **Prediction markets**: Platner 92% on Polymarket, 80-82% on Kalshi to win Democratic primary.
- **Campaign launch video** received ~2.5M views in 24 hours — unprecedented for a first-time Senate candidate.
- **$1M raised in first 9 days** (avg $33); $4.6M in Q4 2025 alone — one of the strongest small-dollar Senate fundraising runs in recent cycles.
